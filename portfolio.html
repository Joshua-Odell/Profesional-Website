Behave

My wife is a Behavior Analysts who works at a school that teaches kids with severe behavior problems.
One struggle is communicating with parents to ensure that the same techniques are being applied at school and at home.
This app will allow parents to log behavior episodes to create an accurate timeline when paired with teacher information.
This will assist the diagnosis of behavior triggers and assist with meaningful progress.

This app will consist of a phone based application as well as a detailed website for the acess of data. This requires full stack work
